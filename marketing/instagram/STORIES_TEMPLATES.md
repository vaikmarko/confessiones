## ğŸ“Š Story Analytics

Track these metrics (Instagram Insights):

**Engagement Metrics:**
- **Views:** How many people saw it
- **Exits:** People who swiped away (bad)
- **Replies:** People who DMed you
- **Sticker Taps:** Polls, questions answered
- **Link Clicks:** If you have swipe-up
- **Profile Visits:** From this story
- **Forward:** People who skipped ahead
- **Back:** People who rewatched

**Goals:**
- High completion rate (watched all the way through)
- Low exits (didn't swipe away)
- High sticker engagement (polls, questions)
- Profile visits & link clicks

**Winners:** Stories with high engagement â†’ create more like these!
