## üìä CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION TIPS

### Copywriting Best Practices
1. **Hook in first 3 seconds** - Address pain immediately
2. **Agitate the problem** - Make them feel understood
3. **Present solution** - Show how you solve their specific pain
4. **Social proof** - Testimonials, stats, Scripture
5. **Strong CTA** - Clear, urgent, benefit-focused
6. **Risk reversal** - "4 FREE conversations", "Anonymous", "No credit card"
