### 2. Paid Social Ads (SCALABLE, Predictable)

**Effort:** 1 hour/week (monitoring)  
**Cost:** $300-1000/month  
**Expected:** 30% of signups Month 1, 50% by Month 6

**Strategy:**
- Month 1: $10-15/day ($300-450/month) - Testing
- Month 2: $20-25/day ($600-750/month) - Scaling winners
- Month 3+: $30-50/day ($900-1500/month) - Full scale

**Ad Types:**
1. **Awareness Ads** (30% of budget)
   - Goal: Get My Confessions in front of Christians
   - Format: Scripture quotes, testimonials
   - Target: Christian interests, mental health interests
   
2. **Traffic Ads** (50% of budget)
   - Goal: Drive website visits
   - Format: Problem-solution, free trial offers
   - Target: Website visitors lookalike, engagers
   
3. **Conversion Ads** (20% of budget)
   - Goal: Premium signups
   - Format: Premium benefits, testimonials
   - Target: Free users retargeting

**Optimization:**
- A/B test everything (audiences, creatives, copy)
- Kill ads with <1% CTR
- Scale ads with >3% CTR
- Target Cost Per Lead: <$15
- Target Cost Per Premium: <$100
