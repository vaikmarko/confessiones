## ğŸ“Š A/B Testing Strategy

**Test these variables:**

1. **Audience:**
   - Catholics vs. General Christians
   - Ages 25-35 vs. 40-55
   - Female vs. Male vs. All

2. **Creative:**
   - Single Image vs. Carousel vs. Video
   - Testimonial vs. Feature highlight vs. Problem-focused
   - Dark vs. Light background
   - With vs. Without emoji

3. **Copy:**
   - Short (50 words) vs. Long (150+ words)
   - Question headline vs. Statement
   - "Start Free" vs. "Learn More" CTA
   - Scripture verse included vs. Not included

4. **Placement:**
   - Facebook Feed only
   - Instagram Feed only
   - Facebook + Instagram combined
   - Stories vs. Feed

**Run tests for 3-7 days, then:**
- Kill losing ad (< 1% CTR)
- Scale winning ad (+50% budget)
- Create variations of winner

### Top 5 Topics to Start With:

2. **Anger in Marriage** - High conversion rate

4. **Loneliness / Single** - Underserved demographic

### Implementation:

**Month 1:** Test top 5 topics
- Budget: $2/day per topic = $10/day total
- Run for 7 days
- Measure: CTR, Cost per Lead, Quality of conversations

**Month 2:** Scale top 3, add 5 more topics
- Scale winners: $5/day each
- Test new topics: $2/day each
- Total budget: $25/day

**Month 3:** Full 20-topic approach
- Top performers: $10/day each
- Medium performers: $3/day each
- Low performers: Pause and refine

### Topic-Specific Targeting:

**Anxiety:** Interests: Anxiety relief, Stress management, Mental health
